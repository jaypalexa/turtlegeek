ROSTER (Record Of Sea Turtles Electronic Reports) is a computer program designed to facilitate the collection and reporting of marine turtle and holding tank data required by the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission (FWC).
MANIFEST (Mote's Automated Nesting Information Forms for Sea Turtles) is a computer program designed to facilitate the collection and transfer of nesting survey data for Mote Marine Laboratory's Sea Turtle Conservation and Research Program.
VAQSTAR (Virginia Aquarium Sea Turtle Attribute Repository) is a computer program designed to collect and report on sea turtle stranding and necropsy data for the Virginia Aquarium and Marine Science Center.
