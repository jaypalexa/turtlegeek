Duper Bowl XXXI

The NFL season is down to the last game and vultures are circling the football field at Mason Mill Park. This can mean only one thing... THE DUPER BOWL!

You are invited to play in the 31st annual Duper Bowl. Touch-football for the old, infirmed and otherwise physically inept. Anyone who can make his/her way to the field by 12:00 PM on Sunday, February 7th 2010 is welcome to participate or just sit in the stands and make fun of those who do.

The game usually lasts a little over an hour then we head to The Famous Pub at Toco Hill for post-game drinks and burgers. Again, anyone is eligible to participate regardless of age, sex, physical frailty or mental instability, so feel free to coerce friends who may be interested.

Best of all It Don't Cost Nuth'n - unless you want to kick in a few bucks to defray printing costs for the much-coveted souvenir jerseys.

Anyone who plans to play please send me an email no later than Monday, February 1st 2010 so I know how many jerseys to have printed (& don't get stuck w/ extras).

Hope to see y'all there!
